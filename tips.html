Before you begin playing...



Press 'E' to proceed.



'E' to Interact



W A S D or arrow keys to move.



Press F11 to go full Screen for the most cohesive experience.



This entire game was made and tested mostly on Firefox so if possible go ahead and try it with firefox. Microsoft edge; the latest one also work best



You can move your mouse cursor around to search for interactable things. It will change its icon to this



This is still a VERY early rough showcase, so it is not even complete in the slightest way. This is just for testing purposes. Everything is bound to change
